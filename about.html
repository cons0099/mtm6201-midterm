Our Story!

“Baker Baker” is a bakery, cafe, and lounge area that is all in one! The name “Baker Baker” is a play on aptronym that is present in our head chef Taylor Baker's name. We strive to be a place of community offering long business hours and tasty treats to keep your day going. Our head chef Taylor Baker has had a love for baking since she was a child. Baking for her family then her friends and eventually she knew she wanted to feed the world.
